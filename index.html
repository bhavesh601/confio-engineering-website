Who We Are

Confio Engineering Solutions Private Limited is a mechanical engineering company engaged in the manufacturing and supply of automotive and industrial components. The company specializes in CNC bending services, sheet metal fabrication, and the production of critical components such as vehicle chassis, gearbox parts, brake discs, industrial sprockets, and wheel hubs. With a strong focus on quality and precision, Confio Engineering Solutions delivers reliable, application-specific solutions to meet diverse industrial requirements.
